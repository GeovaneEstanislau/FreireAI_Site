FreireAI – Autonomous Advertising Optimization

FreireAI is an AI-powered platform designed to help businesses create and optimize digital advertising campaigns. The system analyzes website content, generates multiple ad variations, and automatically adjusts campaign budgets based on performance metrics.

Our platform integrates with the Google Ads API to automate campaign creation, monitor performance indicators such as CTR and CPC, and continuously optimize ad strategies to maximize efficiency while maintaining strict budget control.

FreireAI is designed for educational and small business environments where advertising resources are limited and optimization is critical.
